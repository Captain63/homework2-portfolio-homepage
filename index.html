About Me

Since I was a young child, I have always had an obsession with lighthouses and the ocean at large (Exhibit A: the 40-count model lighthouse collection my grandparents curated for me). You might be wondering what that has to do with a developer portfolio, but as a life-long creative writer, I appreciate the power of a good metaphor. Lighthouses exist to cut through the darkness and guide mariners as they navigate treacherous waters. Such is the goal I aspire to with each web application and site I build. In a world of mediocre designs and unresponsive layouts, I strive for excellence in both presentation and functionality, a shining light through a sea of subpar experiences for the weary user.

In addition to my web developer capabilities, I have an education and background in content writing that allows me to not only build the page but put thought toward what that page should communicate and how to best arrange the elements so that message comes through. As you will see in the following work samples, I have combined both these skillsets to deliver immersive experiences that not only meet usability and responsive standards – but exceed them.
